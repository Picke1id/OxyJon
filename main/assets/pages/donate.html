How You Can Help!

Donations made to LFCA help to keep our community thriving in so many different ways.

LFCA is always investing in programs that will enrich the minds of our children, improve the lives of our elderly, and benefit the community as a whole.

When you make a donation to our organization, portions of those donations go to programs that assist students of Noble Elementary Middle School, other local block clubs that need assistance starting up, and families in need of food and resources in our neighborhoods.

Every dollar counts when you're making a difference, so give responsibly. Any amount you can spare towards our cause is very much felt and appreciated.

Giving also has its' rewards beyond making a difference!

Littlefield Community Association is a 501(c)(3) organization, which means that your contributions can be tax deductible.

(For more information on how your contributions can be tax deductible, speak with your local tax consultant/representative.)
